Our Story

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind Oud Éire

Our Journey

Oud Éire was born from a deep appreciation for the art of perfumery and the mystical allure of oud. Our founder, Liam O'Connor, first encountered oud during his travels through the Middle East and was captivated by its complex, evolving scent profile.

Returning to Ireland, Liam was determined to share this olfactory treasure with others, but with a commitment to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices. Thus, Oud Éire was established in 2015, blending traditional oud craftsmanship with contemporary Irish sensibility.

Today, we work directly with sustainable oud plantations and master perfumers to create fragrances that honor the rich heritage of oud while appealing to modern tastes.

From Sand to Shores – An Essence of a Journey

Our journey began with a simple belief: that scent has the power to connect cultures, evoke memories, and tell stories that transcend borders.

Oud Éire was created to bring the luxury and depth of Dubai's perfumery to the refined soul of Ireland. Our name reflects a meeting of worlds — the golden sands of the East and the wild, misted shores of the West.

Every bottle tells a story of contrast and connection — where Eastern richness meets Western serenity, and tradition evolves into modern elegance.

At Oud Éire, we invite you to discover more than fragrance — to experience an essence of a journey, from sand to shores.

Our Philosophy

At Oud Éire, we believe that fragrance is more than just a scent—it's an experience, a memory, a story waiting to be told. Our philosophy is built on three core principles:

Sustainability

We are committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices, ensuring that our oud is harvested responsibly without harming the environment.

Quality

Every product undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure it meets our high standards for purity, longevity, and scent complexity.

Community

We support the communities where our oud is sourced, providing fair wages and contributing to local development projects.

What is Oud?

Understanding the luxury behind our fragrances

The Origin

Oud, also known as agarwood, is a fragrant dark resinous wood that forms in the heartwood of aquilaria trees when they become infected with a specific type of mold.

The Process

The formation of oud can take many years, making it one of the most expensive raw materials in the world. The resin develops as a defense mechanism against the mold.

Cultural Significance

Oud has been prized for centuries in Middle Eastern, South Asian, and East Asian cultures for its complex scent and spiritual significance.
